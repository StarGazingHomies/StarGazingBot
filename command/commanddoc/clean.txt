Mass deletes messages in a channel.
\*Note: 
When filtering by user, it applies the filter retroactively.
Therefore, it searches through the last 100 messages in the channel
for ones sent by the user, and may delete less.

**Usage:**
`{prefix}clean <amount> [user]`

**Example:**
`{prefix}clean 100`
`{prefix}clean 100 <@!797930119476936715>`\*

**Aliases:**
None
